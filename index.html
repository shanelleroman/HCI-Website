Why Rejoy

The Problem

Selling used goods is frustrating. Calculating the correct price, juggling potential buyers, coordinating item pick-up ... no wonder people send old sweaters to the dumpster! We seek to breathe a second life into used goods.

The Solution

Social Discovery Feed

Shop items popular among your friends

Automatic Pricing

Enter basic information and receive an immediate flat offer + a bonus when your item is sold.

reJoy Box DropOff

Secure boxes are situated conveniently around campus. Type in a code associated with your item and deposit it.
